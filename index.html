F.A.Q.
What is UDGAM?
expand_more
UDGAM is the Annual Entrepreneurship Summit of IIT Guwahati, which is held to bring together the academic community, new-age entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and investors on a common platform.
Would COVID restrictions affect my participation?
expand_more
Don't worry we've got you covered. This edition of E-Summit is completely online and you can attend it from any part of the world!
Is it necessary to participate in a competition to be part of the summit?
expand_more
No, we have separate passes for all those who want to attend speaker sessions, fun events, and workshops.
Who can participate in UDGAM?
expand_more
Students, professionals, professors, or anyone wanting to learn about entrepreneurship and develop their skills all can be a part of the summit.
Will we get recordings of keynotes and panel discussions?
expand_more
Yes, If you have purchased the UDGAM pass and somehow miss the lecture, You’ll be provided with the recordings post the event which you can access forever.
What are the perks of attending the E-Summit?
expand_more
Apart from an awesome learning experience, you are in for some fun activities and a chance to win goodies and certificates from E-Cell IIT Guwahati.
How will I know if my registration is approved?
expand_more
You will receive a mail confirming your registration for Udgam. Make sure you purchased the correct pass. If you don’t find the mail in your inbox please check your spambox.
