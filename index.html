F.A.Q.
Is there any fee to participate in Coalescence?
expand_more
No, your registration will be completely free.
Who has previously come to Coalescence as a speaker?
expand_more
Likes of Nikhil Kamath, Bhuvan Bam, Rajat Sharma and many others have attended Coalescence as speakers. For more information - click here
How long is Coalescence?
expand_more
Coalescence will be hosted for a period of 3 days.
Who hosts Coalescence?
expand_more
Coalescence is hosted by the Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership(CEL) based in BITS-Pilani, KK Birla Goa Campus.
What are the perks of attending the E-Summit?
expand_more
Apart from an awesome learning experience, you are in for some fun activities and a chance to win goodies and certificates from CEL BITS Goa.
Is it necessary to participate in a competition to be part of the summit?
expand_more
No, you can attend talks, workshops, competitons etc. individually as well.
